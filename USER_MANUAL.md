# User Manual

This guide walks through the application one screen at a time and shows where to click for each action. The red box in every picture marks the part being described.

Once the application is running, following SETUP.md, open it in your web browser. Some films show a plain card with the title in place of a poster image. That happens when the poster is no longer available from the image source, and the film still works normally.

## The home page

The home page shows popular movies arranged into rows by genre. The menu along the top moves you between the main pages.

![The home page with the top menu highlighted](docs/manual/01-home.png)

To see more titles in a row, move the pointer over the row and click the arrow on the side.

![A genre row with its scroll arrow highlighted](docs/manual/02-carousel.png)

## Opening a movie

Click any poster to open the details for that film.

![A movie poster highlighted on the home page](docs/manual/03-open-movie.png)

The popup shows the poster, the description, the cast and crew, the rating, the year, and the running time.

![The movie detail popup with the description highlighted](docs/manual/04-detail.png)

The View on IMDb button opens the film's page on the IMDb website in a new tab.

![The detail popup with the IMDb button highlighted](docs/manual/05-imdb.png)

The Recommend similar button opens the recommendation page using this film as the starting point.

![The detail popup with the Recommend similar button highlighted](docs/manual/06-recommend-similar.png)

## Searching

Open the Search page from the top menu.

![The Search item in the top menu highlighted](docs/manual/07-search-nav.png)

Type part of a title. Matching films appear as you type, and clicking one opens its details.

![The search box with results below it](docs/manual/08-search-results.png)

## Creating an account

An account lets you like films, keep a watchlist, and add friends. Click Sign in at the top right.

![The Sign in link in the top menu highlighted](docs/manual/09-signin-link.png)

To make a new account, choose Create an account, enter your name, a username, and a password, then click Create account. If you already have an account, sign in with your username and password instead.

![The registration form with the Create account button highlighted](docs/manual/10-register.png)

Once you are signed in, your name appears at the top right, next to the heart icon and the Log out button.

![The top bar after signing in, with the account area highlighted](docs/manual/11-signed-in.png)

## Getting recommendations

Open the Recommend page. Start by describing what you feel like watching in the box at the top. This is optional.

![The recommendation page with the description box highlighted](docs/manual/12-mood.png)

Choose one or more genres.

![The genre buttons highlighted](docs/manual/13-genres.png)

Narrow the results further by an actor, a director, or a minimum rating.

![The actor, director, and minimum rating fields highlighted](docs/manual/14-filters.png)

If you have added friends, pick the ones whose taste you want included. Adding friends is covered further down.

![The friends selection highlighted on the recommendation page](docs/manual/15-friends.png)

Click Get recommendations.

![The Get recommendations button highlighted](docs/manual/16-get-recommendations.png)

Each result carries a short reason it was chosen, such as a matching genre, a film you liked, or a friend who would recommend it.

![A recommendation result with its reasons highlighted](docs/manual/17-reasons.png)

## Liking movies

Click the heart on any poster to like a film. Click it again to remove the like.

![A poster with its heart button highlighted](docs/manual/18-like.png)

Open the heart icon at the top right to see all of your liked films on one page.

![The liked movies page with the heart icon highlighted](docs/manual/19-liked-page.png)

## Your watchlist

Open a film and click the watchlist button to save it for later.

![The detail popup with the watchlist button highlighted](docs/manual/20-add-watchlist.png)

Open the Watchlist page from the top menu to see your saved films and remove any you no longer want.

![The watchlist page with a remove button highlighted](docs/manual/21-watchlist-page.png)

## Friends

Friends can be other members or the named viewer profiles taken from the ratings data. Their taste can feed into your recommendations. Open the Settings page.

![The Settings item in the top menu highlighted](docs/manual/22-settings-nav.png)

Search for a person by name and click Add to add them.

![The people search with an Add button highlighted](docs/manual/23-add-friend.png)

Your friends are listed on the left. To remove one, click the cross next to their name.

![The friends list with a remove cross highlighted](docs/manual/24-remove-friend.png)
